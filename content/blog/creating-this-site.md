I have been wanting to update my personal website for a while. Since today is Memorial Day, I had some free time, so I decided to spend it creating this site.

The other reason is that I wanted to read more. By creating this site, I can use it to jot down notes about the things I am reading.

The goal for this site is straightforward. I would like to use it as a way to collect notes on what I am reading, short posts about what I am learning, and occasional updates on things I am trying to work on.

This site is meant to be simple and not too fancy. The goal is to make the content here easy to digest.

I look forward to coming back to this post in a few years from now to see how much progress I've made since the creation of this page.

For now, this marks my first blog post. 🥳
